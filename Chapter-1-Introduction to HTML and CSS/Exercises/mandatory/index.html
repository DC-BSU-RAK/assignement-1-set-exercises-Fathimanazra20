Butter Chicken

Butter chicken is a rich and creamy Indian dish...

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into cubes
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

How to make

  1. Marinate the Chicken: In a bowl, combine the chicken pieces with yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, garam masala, turmeric powder, chili powder, and salt. Mix well, ensuring all pieces are coated. Cover and let it marinate for at least 30 minutes, or up to 4 hours in the refrigerator.
  2. Cook the Chicken: Melt 1 tablespoon of butter in a large skillet or pot over medium-high heat. Add the marinated chicken and cook until it's browned on all sides. Remove the chicken from the pan and set it aside.
  3. Saut√© the Onion: In the same skillet, melt the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter. Add the finely chopped onion and cook until it turns golden brown, about 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add Tomatoes and Simmer: Pour in the crushed tomatoes and cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, allowing the tomatoes to reduce slightly.
  5. Combine and Simmer with Cream: Return the cooked chicken to the skillet. Pour in the heavy cream, and bring the mixture to a simmer. Cook for about 10 minutes, or until the sauce thickens slightly and the chicken is fully cooked.
  6. Finish and Serve: Stir well to combine. Garnish with chopped cilantro and serve hot with rice or naan bread.
